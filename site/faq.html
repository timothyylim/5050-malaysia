FAQs

What’s the point of 50-50 Malaysia?

50-50 Malaysia is a volunteer-driven initiative spotlighting women experts in various industries and tackling under-representation head-on. We provide details of women experts in different industries, specify their specialisations, and provide email addresses. There’s no excuse for a lack of gender balance in panels and forums.

Can you vouch for every expert listed on this website?

Usual disclaimers apply here – although we have done some due diligence, we unfortunately cannot and do not know everything about everyone. We also stress that the opinions and views of the experts platformed here do not necessarily reflect the official policy or position of 50-50 Malaysia. We will not be liable for the accuracy of information spread by these experts and any subsequent losses or damages in connection with the use of our website.

You have platformed someone who has acted abusively towards me or someone else. How do I put in a complaint?

We at 50-50 Malaysia take complaints about issues like sexual harassment, cyber-bullying and abusive behaviour very seriously. Please write in to fiftyfiftymalaysia@gmail.com so we can investigate the matter. Rest assured that all complaints will remain confidential.

I would like to be listed on 50-50 Malaysia. How do I go about this?

Thank you for the support! There is a Google Form you can fill out here. The website is updated every two months.

I am listed on this website and I’d like some details edited or amended. How can I get in touch?

Email us at fiftyfiftymalaysia@gmail.com and we’ll get this sorted! We also reach out to listed profiles once a year to ask if they would like to update any details, but this process is time-consuming: it’s far quicker to get in touch independently.

I want to be listed on this website but my industry is not listed. How do we fix this?

There is a degree of granularity we seek to avoid when listing industries. For example, if you are an environment journalist you will be categorised under Journalism and Media. We will not create a separate listing for Environment Journalism. That said, we are consistently updating and adding industry categories to better describe listed experts. In November 2025 we added several including Philanthropy, Fashion, and Animal Rights, as well as decoupling Natural Sciences and Mathematics. If we are missing your industry please get in touch with us at fiftyfiftymalaysia@gmail.com.

I am being cyber-bullied because of details published on this website. What do I do?

Women are often the targets of online violence. This is unacceptable. Send us an email and we’ll support you throughout an individualised remedy process – whether it’s getting you in touch with the relevant authorities, helping you write official complaint letters, or connecting you with support groups. We are also happy to remove you from the database if that is your preferred solution.

I really like this initiative. How can I support 50-50 Malaysia?

Aww, thanks! We like you too. Right now this is a not-for-profit volunteer project so we are not seeking financial assistance. You can show your support in other ways: sharing the website with your mates, praising us on social media, or maybe just taking us out for a drink.

I have a super specific question that isn’t listed in this FAQ. How do I get in touch?

Send us a letter via pigeon. Just kidding. Email us at fiftyfiftymalaysia@gmail.com.
