# 50-50 Malaysia — Context

> Source: https://5050malaysia.com/ (About Us: https://5050malaysia.com/about-us/)
> Captured: 2026-07-06

## What it is

50-50 Malaysia is a searchable **database platform of women experts** dedicated to
amplifying women's voices in Malaysian public discourse. It serves as a resource
for **policymakers, journalists, and the general public** who are seeking female
expertise, with the goal of promoting **gender equality and diversity** in public
forums, panels, and news coverage.

## Mission / Purpose

- Make it easy to find qualified women experts across a wide range of fields.
- Counter the under-representation of women on panels, in forums, and in news
  pieces ("manels").
- Promote gender equality and diversity in Malaysian public discourse.

## How it works

- The database catalogs women experts across **40+ professional sectors** relevant
  to Malaysia.
- Users can **search the database** and **browse by industry category**.
- Positioned as an accessible tool for anyone seeking diverse expert perspectives
  on contemporary Malaysian issues.
