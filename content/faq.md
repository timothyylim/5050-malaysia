    question: "What’s the point of 50-50 Malaysia?"
    answer: "50-50 Malaysia is a volunteer-driven initiative spotlighting women experts in various industries and tackling under-representation head-on. We provide details of women experts in different industries, specify their specialisations, and provide email addresses. There’s no excuse for a lack of gender balance in panels and forums."

    question: "Can you vouch for every expert listed on this website?"

We also stress that the opinions and views of the experts platformed here do not necessarily reflect the official policy or position of 50-50 Malaysia. We will not be liable for the accuracy of information spread by these experts and any subsequent losses or damages in connection with the use of our website."

    question: "I am listed on this website and I’d like some details edited or amended. How can I get in touch?"

We also reach out to listed profiles once a year to ask if they would like to update any details, but this process is time-consuming: it’s far quicker to get in touch independently."

    question: "I want to be listed on this website but my industry is not listed. How do we fix this?"
    answer: "There is a degree of granularity we seek to avoid when listing industries. For example, if you are an environment journalist you will be categorised under Journalism and Media. We will not create a separate listing for Environment Journalism. That said, we are consistently updating and adding industry categories to better describe listed experts. In November 2025 we added several including Philanthropy, Fashion, and Animal Rights, as well as decoupling Natural Sciences and Mathematics. If we are missing your industry please get in touch with us at [fiftyfiftymalaysia@gmail.com](mailto:fiftyfiftymalaysia@gmail.com)."

We are also happy to remove you from the database if that is your preferred solution."
